Signal · market monitoring agent

An agent that watches your space, so your Monday brief writes itself.

Tell Signal what you're tracking. Once a day, it searches the web, throws out the noise, and drops a short, human email with the 3 or 4 things that actually matter.

How it works

01

You tell it your industry and what kind of moves you care about — launches, funding, hires, pricing, competitors.

02

It searches the web, then judges what's genuinely worth your time versus what's just noise.

03

You get a short, curated email. Three or four things, never a wall of links.

Set up your watch

Free text, as short or as long as you need. Broad theme, a specific niche, whatever you'd tell a sharp analyst to go look into.

Add names if you already know who to track. Skip it if you want Signal to also surface companies you don't know about yet.
